# Authentication

Sign in to your Jellyfin account.



## Username & Password

The standard sign-in method:

1. Enter your Jellyfin username
2. Enter your password (leave blank if none)
3. Press **Sign In**



## Quick Connect

Sign in without typing your password on the TV.

**Steps:**

1. Select **Quick Connect** on the sign-in screen
2. Note the 6-digit code displayed
3. On another device, open Jellyfin web interface
4. Go to **Settings** > **Quick Connect**
5. Enter the code and approve
6. Neptune signs you in automatically

**Note:** Quick Connect must be enabled on your server. Contact your administrator if you see an error.



## Session Storage

Neptune securely saves your session:

- Authentication tokens stored in device Keychain
- Sessions persist across app restarts
- Quick profile switching without re-entering password

See [Profiles](/personalization/profiles) for managing multiple accounts.
