# Initial Setup

Neptune guides you through a simple setup process to get started.

---

## Setup Steps

| Step | Description |
|------|-------------|
| [Server Connection](/getting-started/setup/server-connection) | Connect to your Jellyfin server |
| [Authentication](/getting-started/setup/authentication) | Sign in to your account |
| [Plugins](/getting-started/setup/plugins) | Optional server plugins |
| [Jellyseerr](/getting-started/setup/jellyseerr) | Optional content discovery |

---

## Quick Overview

1. **Connect** - Neptune finds your Jellyfin server automatically or you enter it manually
2. **Sign In** - Use username/password or Quick Connect
3. **Plugins** - Neptune detects and configures compatible server plugins
4. **Jellyseerr** - Optionally connect for content discovery and requests
5. **Theme** - Choose your visual theme

After setup, you'll land on the home screen ready to browse your library.
