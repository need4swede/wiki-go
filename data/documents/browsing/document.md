## Overview

Neptune provides multiple ways to discover and browse your media:

### Home Screen

Your personalized landing page featuring:

- **Hero Spotlight**: Large carousel highlighting what to watch next
- **Continue Watching**: Resume where you left off
- **Next Up**: Next episodes in your shows
- **Recently Added**: New content in your library
- **Recommendations**: Personalized suggestions

[Learn more about the Home Screen →](/browsing/home-screen)

### Movies & Shows Tabs

Filtered views of your library:

- **Movies tab**: All movie content with movie-specific sections
- **Shows tab**: All TV content with show-specific sections

### Item Details

When you select any movie or show:

- Full metadata and overview
- Cast and crew information
- For TV shows: Season and episode browsing
- Extras and special features
- Related content and collections

[Learn more about Item Details →](/browsing/item-details)

### Search

Find content across your entire library:

- Instant results as you type
- Search by title, genre, actor, director, or composer
- Results from both library and Jellyseerr (if connected)

[Learn more about Search →](/browsing/search)

---

## Tips

- **Swipe down** from the menu bar to quickly access content
- **Long-press** items for quick actions
- Use **Search** for the fastest way to find specific content
- Customize your **Home Screen** to show sections you care about
