# Movies

Movie detail pages show everything about a film.



## Top Section

- **Play button**: Start playback (shows "Resume" if in progress)
- **Progress bar**: Watch progress indicator
- **Menu button**: Additional options



## Media Badges

Color-coded badges show video and audio quality.

**Video:**

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 4K (Green) | 2160p resolution |
| 1080p | Full HD |
| DV (Purple) | Dolby Vision HDR |
| HDR10 (Orange) | HDR10 format |

**Audio:**

| Badge | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| Atmos (Blue) | Dolby Atmos |
| DTS:X (Red) | DTS:X audio |
| Lossless (Gold) | TrueHD, DTS-HD MA |



## Content Sections

| Section | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Cast & Crew** | Actors, director, production team |
| **Extras** | Trailers, behind-the-scenes, featurettes |
| **Collections** | Related movie collections |
| **Similar** | Movies with similar themes |

Select a person to see their other work in your library.
